Books by Abdus Salam


Abdus Salam, the Nobel Prize-winning physicist, penned notable works such as "Selected Papers of Abdus Salam" (1994), a compilation showcasing his influential contributions to theoretical physics. "Ideals and Realities: Selected Essays of Abdus Salam" (1987) provides a glimpse into his philosophical perspectives on science and education. His pivotal paper "A Grand Unified Theory of Weak and Electromagnetic Interactions" (1968), co-authored with Glashow and Weinberg, laid the groundwork for the electroweak unification in the Standard Model. Despite facing challenges due to discrimination, Salam's writings continue to inspire and shape the understanding of fundamental forces in nature.
